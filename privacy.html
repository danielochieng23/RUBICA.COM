Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection are our priorities

Last updated: December 2024
Privacy Commitment: Rubica.com is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when:

  • Creating an account or profile
  • Posting advertisements or content
  • Contacting our support team
  • Subscribing to newsletters or notifications
  • Making payments for premium services

1.2 Types of Data Collected

Data Type Examples Purpose
Contact Information Name, email, phone number, address Account creation, communication, verification
Profile Information Age, description, preferences, photos Profile creation, ad posting, matching
Payment Information Payment method, billing address Processing payments, refunds
Usage Data IP address, browser type, pages visited Website improvement, security
Device Information Device type, operating system, unique identifiers Technical support, optimization

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Log data (IP addresses, browser information, timestamps)
  • Cookies and tracking technologies
  • Usage patterns and website interactions
  • Location data (if permitted)
  • Device and browser characteristics

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Uses

  • Service Provision: Create and maintain your account, display your ads, process transactions
  • Communication: Send important updates, respond to inquiries, provide customer support
  • Safety & Security: Verify identities, prevent fraud, ensure community guidelines compliance
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns, improve website functionality, develop new features
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

2.2 Marketing and Communications

  • Send promotional emails and newsletters (with your consent)
  • Notify you about relevant ads and services
  • Provide personalized recommendations
  • Market research and user feedback collection

2.3 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Your consent for marketing communications and optional features
  • Contract performance for providing our services
  • Legitimate interests for website improvement and security
  • Legal obligations for compliance and safety requirements

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 When We Share Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Public Profile Information: Information you choose to make public in your ads or profile
  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate our website (hosting, payment processing, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety and Protection: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

3.2 Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party providers for:

  • Payment processing (encrypted and secure)
  • Email services for communications
  • Analytics to improve user experience
  • Cloud storage for data security
  • Fraud prevention and security monitoring

3.3 Information Not Shared

We never share your personal information for:

  • Third-party marketing without your explicit consent
  • Data selling or revenue generation
  • Unauthorized commercial purposes
  • Non-essential business operations

4. Data Security and Protection

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access controls and authentication
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Protected servers and secure data centers
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and incident response

4.2 Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Ad data: Retained according to package duration
  • Payment data: Retained for accounting and legal requirements
  • Log data: Typically retained for 12 months
  • Deleted data: Permanently removed within 30 days of deletion request

4.3 Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Report to relevant authorities as required by law
  • Take immediate steps to secure the breach
  • Provide clear information about the incident and next steps

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you

Right to Rectification

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data

Right to Restrict

Limit how we process your personal data

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a portable format

Right to Object

Object to certain types of data processing

5.1 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@rubica.com
  • Response time: Within 30 days
  • Identity verification may be required
  • No charge for most requests

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is strictly for users 18 years and older. We do not:

  • Knowingly collect information from anyone under 18
  • Target advertising to minors
  • Allow registration by users under 18
  • Permit content involving minors

If we become aware that a user under 18 has provided personal information, we will immediately delete their account and all associated data.
